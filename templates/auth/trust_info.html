🔐 Understanding Your Trust Score

The Trust Score reflects the system's confidence in your identity and behavior during login. It helps protect sensitive data by enforcing security policies dynamically.

🧮 How the Trust Score Is Calculated
  • ✅ Multi-Factor Authentication: Using TOTP or WebAuthn increases your trust.
  • 📍 Known Device & IP: Logging in from familiar devices improves trust.
  • ❌ Failed Logins: Multiple failed attempts lower your trust score.
  • 🆕 New Devices: First-time logins from unrecognized environments reduce trust.
  • 🔐 Custom Rules: Your organization may apply additional trust policies.
🔒 How Trust Score Affects Access
  • 🩺 View Sensitive Records: Requires trust ≥ 0.7
  • ⚠️ Medium Trust: You may be required to complete additional verification
  • 🚫 Low Trust: Some actions may be blocked until trust is improved
Tip: You can improve your trust score by enabling MFA, using familiar devices, and avoiding suspicious login behavior.
